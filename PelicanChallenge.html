Pelican Challenge

Controls

Click the game to give it focus.

Dive to catch fish. If you dive from further up, you dive deeper.

Normal fish give you 1 point, the larger ones give you 2.

If you hit a jellyfish, you loose 5 seconds of your precious time, but you get 5 additional ones if you catch a pink fish.

  • SPACE: Dive
  • LEFT: Move to the left
  • RIGHT: Move to the right
  • UP: Move up
  • DOWN: Move down
